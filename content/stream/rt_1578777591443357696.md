I think designers underestimate just how good, usable, inclusive, &amp; sometimes even refreshingly delightful "boring" design is.

- Visible labels &amp; instructions.
- Link underlines!
- Good text contrast.
- Lists!
- Links! (to separate pages vs. modal dialogs)

Usable &gt; aesthetic.
